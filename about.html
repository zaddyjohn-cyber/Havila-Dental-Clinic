About Havila Dental Clinic

Where every smile matters — trusted, compassionate dental care in the heart of Anthony, Lagos.

Our Story

Trusted Care, Right Here in Lagos

Havila Dental Clinic was founded with a single, clear mission: to make premium-quality dental care accessible and affordable to families across Anthony, Lagos and beyond. We believed — and still believe — that excellent oral health should not be a luxury reserved for the few.

Since opening our doors, we have built a reputation as one of the most trusted dental practices in the Anthony community. Our approach is straightforward: listen to the patient first, diagnose carefully, recommend honestly, and treat with skill. No unnecessary procedures. No hidden costs. Just honest, compassionate dentistry.

We have invested in modern dental equipment and sterilisation protocols to ensure every visit is safe, clean, and comfortable. Whether you're coming in for a routine check-up or a complex smile transformation, you will always be treated with the same care and respect.

Our Core Values

These three pillars guide every decision we make — from how we treat our patients to how we run our clinic.

🤝

Integrity

We believe in honest communication — always. We will never recommend a treatment you don't need, and we will always explain your options clearly so you can make an informed decision. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden surprises.

❤️

Compassion

We understand that visiting the dentist can feel daunting. Our entire team is trained to be patient, empathetic, and gentle. We move at your pace, answer your questions, and ensure you feel comfortable every step of the way.

⭐

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest clinical and service standards. We invest in training, technology, and quality materials so that every treatment we deliver is safe, effective, and long-lasting. Good enough is never good enough for us.
