Who We Are

Where Expertise Meets Empathy

Havila Dental Clinic was founded with one purpose: to make exceptional dental care accessible, comfortable, and personalised for every patient who walks through our doors.

Located on Oyedele Ogunniyi Street in Anthony, Lagos, we serve individuals and families across the city with a wide range of dental services — from routine cleanings to complex restorations and smile transformations.

We invest in the latest dental technology and continue our professional education so we can always deliver the highest standard of care — with warmth and patience every step of the way.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Patient-Centred Care

Every decision we make starts with what's best for you. We take time to listen, explain, and involve you in your own care.

Clinical Excellence

We maintain the highest clinical standards through continuous learning and investment in modern dental technology.

Safety First

Hospital-grade sterilisation and strict infection control protocols are non-negotiable at Havila. Your safety is guaranteed.

Comfort & Calm

We design every aspect of your visit — from the waiting room to the treatment chair — to minimise anxiety and maximise comfort.

Community Focus

We are proud to serve the Anthony and wider Lagos community — building lasting relationships with families across generations.

Transparent Pricing

No surprise bills. We explain all costs clearly before treatment so you can make informed decisions with full confidence.
